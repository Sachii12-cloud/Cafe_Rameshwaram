ABOUT US

About Us

The Rameshwaram Cafe


The Rameshwaram Cafe is a premium South Indian chain of QSR module and is a trademark registered brand under the mother company M/s. Altran Ventures Pvt. Ltd. At The Rameshwaram Café it is ensured that all the cuisines are prepared on-the-go and served hot to customers using the best and fresh high-quality ingredients, authentic recipes with the highest hygiene standards.

What makes The Rameshwaram Cafe unique?
The tastes and the quality of the food is consistent across all the outlets. With uncompromised quality, it’s one-of-a-kind South Indian Cafe that’s open from 6.30 AM to 1 AM throughout and caters to people across all strata.


Our Founders

Mr.Raghavendra Rao

"They both started ‘The Rameshwaram Cafe’ with the aim to provide original, unfiltered South Indian flavours globally. Over the years, both the founders have received many awards and accolades for their impeccable contribution in their respective fields."

Inspiration

The Man Who Inspired Many


“You have to dream before your dreams can come true.”


“Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts, and thoughts result in action”


- Dr A. P. J. Abdul Kalam


It was dream of founders to launch “The Rameshwaram Café” as a premium pure vegetarian South Indian Chain of Quick Service Restaurant. That dream transformed and came true during 2021 with successful launch of two outlets in Bengaluru. The name ‘Rameshwaram’ was chosen by founders to pay tribute to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam as it was his birthplace. It holds true to its value of authenticity and the expansive flavours of South India. In the chaotic cacophony of extrovert leaders, Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam led the nation with his patience, dedication, and charming old-school ways. At Rameshwaram, we look up to him in every form and draw inspiration from the way he lived — holding ground in grandeur.

Our Goals

At The Rameshwaram Cafe, we strive to make South Indian flavours accessible in their most authentic form beyond Bangalore and travel to cities like Chennai, Hyderabad, Delhi, Pune, Ahmedabad and Mumbai, with a strong global presence in the next 5 years.

In India, with varied cultures, religions and traditions, our food industry connects people within cities, within states, and within nations. With shared heritage and stories of the yore, wherever we are, our food reminds us of our roots and this emotion lies close to our heart. At The Rameshwaram Cafe, we make sure to keep that emotion alive, and feed that emotion happily!
